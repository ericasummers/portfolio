Drewpedia Webpage Project

For this pair project, Erica and her partner David R. needed to recreate the main Wikipedia page from scratch in order to learn how to achieve the relevant stylistic effects and how to build the layout.

The final product at end of day for this page looked as follows:

The page was created in steps with the following tools:

  • CSS
